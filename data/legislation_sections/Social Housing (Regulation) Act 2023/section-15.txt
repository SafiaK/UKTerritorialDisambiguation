15 Notification requirements: expansion to profit-making organisations In the Housing and Regeneration Act 2008, in the following provisions omit “non-profit”— (a) section 160(1) (arrangements and reconstructions); (b) section 161(1) (conversion of registered company to registered society); (c) section 163(1) (restructuring of registered society); (d) section 165(1) (dissolution of registered society); (e) section 169A (change of rules of registered society); (f) section 169C (change of articles of registered company).
